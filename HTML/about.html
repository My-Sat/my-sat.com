About Us

My-Sat is an online platform for sale of satellite products and satellite services, products are ordered and deliveries are made to customer's doorstep before payments are made. Products odered within Tamale and its sub-villages are paid exactly as the price seen on the site. Customers who are a bit distanced from Tamale will have to pay for transport if number of product(s) odered do not match agreed quantity.

This site also run services to customers who want to setup their satellite for the first time. Ranging from indoor (mounting of TV) to outdoor (mounting of satellite dishes) or who have issues with their already setup satellites.

This site also have useful resources for old and new satellite installers, under the 'track satellite link, there are resources to guide installers.
